  2. Wait k (delayed VRF)
  3. Challenge → Prove → Verify and Seal
  4. Fraud Window → Finalize

2) ASR – Auto-Selection Router

  • Suitability: Match with job requirement
  • Performance: Reliability/statistics (EWMA rolling 7–30d)
  • FairnessBoost: Quota limits, newcomer ε-greedy boost, anti-collusion by org/ASN/region
  • Key parameters: K, q_max, ε (DAO-defined)

3) Posted Price Controller (PPC)

  • Adaptive price per round to prevent congestion & keep stability
  • DAO: α, β, min/max price bounds
